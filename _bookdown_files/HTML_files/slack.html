Welcome To Silva21

2 Slack

Within the Silva21 Project and across every institution and partner involved with it, we use Slack for instant communication. Slack is an effective way to communicate with colleagues within various channels about any topic of interest. We use it for event planning and organization, networking with peers, and develop a community promoting the sharing of resources and knowledge.

The Silva21 Slack portal ID is silva21.slack.com.

The Silva21 Slack contains several channels, each serving different purposes. The most important channels are:

  • #général - For general discussions on various topics

  • #SITE_hub_site - For discussions and news related to an individual hub site

  • #data - To discuss, share, plan, and locate datasets
